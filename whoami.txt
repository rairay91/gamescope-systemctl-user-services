In terminal type following command=>
loginctl show-session $(loginctl | grep $(whoami) | awk '{print $1}') -p Type -p Desktop -p Id




On terminal you will see this output=>
Id=1
Desktop=gamescope
Type=wayland

Id=2
Desktop=
Type=unspecified
